Welcome

I am a 2nd year Master's student working as a Research Assistant supervised by Prof. K Madhava Krishna at Robotics Research Centre, International Institute of Information Technology-Hyderabad, India.

I am working on Indoor Scene Understanding and inference to enable robots in searching for objects in indoor scenes. Earlier, I worked towards developing a framework to search and discover small objects scattered over large indoor floors. This work was published at ICRA-14, ICPR-14 and ICVGIP-14.

My interests revolve around Computer vision and Machine Learning. Lately I have developed a lot of interest in Deep Neural Networks and their applications.

I earlier interned at Siemens Research, India, National University of Singapore and Notion Ink Design Labs, India. I had a great time at those places and explored stuff like Spiking Neural Networks, Deep Neural Networks, Knowledge Based Systems, etc.

I love food. Cooking gives me loads of bliss and eating as well. I am crazy about musrooms and food that has mushrooms in it. I love travelling. I ve recently been on a road trip covering 1000 KM in Uttaranchal Hill ranges in Northern Himlayas.

Welcome

I am working as an Intern at Computer Vision Lab(CVLD), supervised by Prof. Carsten Rother and Dr. Michael Yang at Technische Universität Dresden. Earlier, I did my Master's under the guidance of Prof. K Madhava Krishna at Robotics Research Centre, International Institute of Information Technology-Hyderabad, India.

I am working on Outdoor Scene Understanding. During my Master's, I worked towards developing a framework to search and discover small objects scattered over large indoor floors. This work was published at ICRA-14, ICPR-14, ICVGIP-14 and ECMR-15.

My interests revolve around Computer vision and Machine Learning. Lately I have developed a lot of interest in Deep Neural Networks and their applications. I earlier interned at Siemens Research, India, National University of Singapore and Notion Ink Design Labs, India.

Cooking gives me loads of bliss. I am crazy about mushrooms and food that has them. I love travelling. Recently I went on a road trip covering 1000 KM in Uttaranchal Hill ranges in Southern Himlayas in India on a Royal Enfield Thunderbird.

Welcome

Hello!! I am a 2nd year Master's student working as a Research Assistant supervised by Prof. K Madhava Krishna at Robotics Research Centre, International Institute of Information Technology-Hyderabad, India.

I am currently working on Indoor Scene Understanding and inference to enable robots in searching for objects in indoor scenes. Earlier, I worked on a framework to search and discover small objects scattered over large indoor floors. This work has been published at ICRA-14, ICPR-14 and ICVGIP-14. Please do have a look at >Research and Publications page for details.

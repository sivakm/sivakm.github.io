### Face Recognition using Deep Neural Nets
<font size="2">
I am developing a Deep Belief Network based Face Recognition architecture for Frueh and Partner-Personal Robotics. This system along with a GUI will be ported onto the robot PRob(Personal Robot) for view and illumination invariant face recognition for personalized robotic assistance. They are located in Zurich and a wonderful team to work with. I got to visit them in 2014 for a month and also represented them at InnoRobo-2014, Lyon and ICRA-2014, HongKong.

I have developed a great interest in Deep Neural Nets since I have been working on this project.
